Blossh is a fragrant expression of duality, representing the bold nature of the modern man. At once strong and sensitive, confident and relaxed, the Blossh embraces his contrasting characteristics with assured ease. Blossh Eau de Toilette is a fragrance of layered notes inspired by the Blossh’s complexity and harmoniously combined for a distinctive blend of modern sophistication.
Blossh is inspired by Carolina Herrera’s unique vision of the duality of the modern woman: audacious and sexy, elegant and enigmatic, good and bad. Forever pushing and crossing the limit, the mysterious sensuality of Blossh makes for a modern yet sophisticated fragrance.
